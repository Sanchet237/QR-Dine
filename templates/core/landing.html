Features

Everything your restaurant needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Instant QR Code

Your QR code is ready the second you register. Print it, stick it on tables. Done. Customers scan with their phone camera—that's it.

Real-Time Updates

Ran out of butter chicken? Mark it unavailable from your phone. Price changed? Update it instantly. Your menu stays current without reprinting a single page.

Mobile-First Design

Works on any phone, loads super fast. Clean design that makes browsing easy. Your customers won't need help figuring it out.

Smart Categories

Sort dishes your way—starters, mains, desserts, drinks. Add filters for veg/non-veg. Let customers search for what they want. Simple.

Photo Uploads

Snap photos of your dishes and upload them straight from your phone. Pictures sell food better than words ever will.

Contactless & Hygienic

No more grimy menu cards passed around all day. Everyone views the menu on their own phone. Cleaner for you, safer for them.
